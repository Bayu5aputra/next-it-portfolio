## Building the City of the Future

Smart cities leverage data and digital technology to improve the quality of life for citizens, enhance sustainability, and streamline urban services.

### Core Components

*   **Smart Infrastructure:** Connected streetlights, waste management systems, and public Wi-Fi.
*   **Data Analytics:** Utilizing big data to optimize city operations and resource allocation.
*   **Citizen Engagement:** Platforms for residents to report issues and participate in decision-making.

Successful implementation requires a clear vision, strong leadership, and collaboration between public and private sectors.
